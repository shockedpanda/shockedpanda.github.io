Product roadmap

All tools share the same philosophy: local first, human in the loop, no vendor lock‑in.

Tool Description Status Target release
KYC Document Certification Validator Automatically checks certification wording, dates, and certifier details — flags non‑compliant documents before human review. In development Q3 2026
STR/SAR Drafting Assistant Analyses transaction data, flags unusual patterns, and drafts suspicious transaction report narratives for MLRO review. Planned Q4 2026
Compliance Librarian Chat‑based assistant that retrieves documents from your secure store, checks expiration dates, and assembles submission packs. Planned Q1 2027
Ongoing Monitoring Engine Periodically re‑screens clients against updated sanctions/PEP lists and adverse media – reports only what’s changed. Research Q2 2027
Corporate Documents Librarian Chat‑based assistant for retrieving and assembling corporate submission packs. Planned Q3 2027
Board/MI Report Compiler Pulls data from multiple compliance sources and drafts ready‑to‑review board packs and management information reports. Planned Q4 2027
Regulatory Change Impact Assessor Maps new regulatory requirements directly to your internal controls — shows exactly which controls need updating. Planned 2028

Note: All dates are estimates and may shift based on pilot feedback and feature prioritisation. We release updates iteratively – you pay only for what you use.
